# Pizza-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI-Project

##  Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI**. The goal of the project is to analyze pizza sales data and extract meaningful business insights from **48,000+ orders** using multiple relational datasets.

The dashboard helps identify **revenue trends, customer ordering patterns, and top-performing products**, enabling data-driven decision making.

---

##  Key Business Questions Answered

### Overview Analysis

* What is the **total revenue generated**?
* How many **total orders** were placed?
* How many **total pizzas** were sold?
* What is the **average order value**?
* What is the **month-wise revenue trend**?
* How are **orders distributed by day of the week**?
* What is the **category-wise sales distribution**?
* Which are the **top 5 pizzas by revenue**?

---

### Sales Analysis

* How many pizzas were sold **by category**?
* How many pizzas were sold **by pizza size**?
* Which are the **top 10 pizzas by quantity sold**?
* Which are the **bottom 10 pizzas by quantity sold**?
* How are orders distributed **by hour of the day**?
* What is the difference between **weekday and weekend sales**?
* What is the **daily pizza sales trend**?
* What is the **average number of pizzas per order**?

---

### Product Performance Analysis

* Which are the **top 10 pizzas by revenue**?
* Which are the **bottom 10 pizzas by revenue**?
* How does **revenue vary across pizza categories**?
* How does **revenue vary across pizza sizes**?
* How does **product performance change over time**?
* What is the **order share for each pizza category**?

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
* **Data Modeling (Star Schema)**
* **Data Visualization**

---

##  Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards for **Revenue, Orders, Pizzas Sold, and Average Order Value**
* **Revenue Trend Analysis by Month**
* **Orders by Day and Hour**
* **Category-wise and Size-wise Sales Distribution**
* **Top & Bottom Performing Products**
* Interactive **Slicers (Filters)** for:

  * Month
  * Category
  * Pizza Size
  * Day of Week
  * Pizza Name

---

##  Key Insights

* Identified **peak sales months and busiest ordering days**
* Discovered **top-performing pizzas driving revenue**
* Analyzed **customer ordering patterns by hour**
* Compared **weekday vs weekend sales performance**
* Evaluated **product performance across categories and sizes**

---

##  Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how **Power BI can transform raw sales data into actionable business insights** using data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visualizations.
